NEW HORIZON HEALTHCARE INC.

Who are we?

New Horizon Healthcare Inc. (NHH) was founded on the beliefs that a local healthcare staffing agency can break the stereotype that is currently circulating throughout the healthcare community. At New Horizon Healthcare , we provide a quality service while meeting the needs of our clients both professionally, financially and spiritually.

Owned and operated by a Canadian family, we are a private health care service agency which will meet your health care needs around the clock and those of your family. New horizon Healthcare Inc. provides a variety of services to help you recover and regain your independence.

New Horizon Healthcare is a company based in Ottawa Ontario. Our standards and expectations guarantee quality service that goes hand in hand with the highest quality client care.

How can we help?

We offer the following services:

  • Personal support services: assistance with daily living like toileting, changing, dressing and feeding
  • Companion service with trained worker who will be with you or family member for safety and security
  • Patient Sitter- trained worker to sit with your family member when you are not available
  • Home support services with light housekeeping, laundry and ,eat preparation
    • We provide relief staffing for all health care facilities
    • We will take the stress out of those last-minute sick calls with well trained, compassionate staff to ensure your patients and residents are cared for .

How do we do this?

We hire the best care givers to give the highest quality care. We hand select our employees to match your care needs. We have a front line scheduling coordinator available 24x7x365 days a year. We truly live by our slogan caring hearts, exceptional people

NHHS believes that excellent patient care is delivered only if the staffing agency goals involve continuous professional growth beginning with our support staff outreaching to our caregivers. We are proud to provide continuing education courses so we can assure our caregivers skills are current and on the cutting edge of new technology and procedures. Our caregivers enjoy excellent work conditions.
